About CampusConnect

A centralized platform for students to share opinions, vote on polls, and engage with campus life through memes.

Our Features

Interactive Polls

Vote on campus-related questions and view real-time results with engaging visuals.

Meme Sharing

Upload, view, and enjoy memes that capture the fun of campus life.

Engaged Community

Connect with fellow students, see trending opinions, and stay in the loop.

Our Journey

2023 - Idea Conception

The concept of CampusConnect was born to unify student engagement on campus.

2024 - Prototype Launch

Initial prototype released; students started using polls and memes features.

2025 - Full Release

Fully functional web app launched with polls, memes, dark mode, and responsive design.
